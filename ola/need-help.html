Need Help

If you need any help regarding booking, ride details, or payments, feel free to check the FAQs below or reach out to our support team:

  • How to book a ride? You can easily book a ride by entering your location and destination in the booking form on the home page.
  • How to cancel a ride? You can cancel a ride from the 'My Rides' section before the driver arrives. A cancellation fee may apply.
  • Payment options and refunds: Ola offers multiple payment options, including cash, card, and Ola Money. For refunds, please contact our support team.
  • Contact support for more help: Reach out to us through the 'Support' section for any issues with your rides, payments, or app.
