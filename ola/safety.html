Safety at Ola

Your safety is our top priority. Ola has implemented several safety measures to ensure that every ride is as safe as possible.

Sanitized Cars

All our vehicles are thoroughly sanitized after every ride to ensure a hygienic environment for passengers.

Mandatory Masks

Both drivers and passengers are required to wear masks at all times during the ride.

Contactless Payments

Use our multiple payment options to pay without any physical contact. Safe and easy!

Emergency Button

If you ever feel unsafe, use the emergency button to alert our support team immediately.
